LITTLE THINGS ADD UP TO BIG THINGS

“He’s someone that can look at your individual game and pick out some things that you can add or something you can work on.”

SIDNEY CROSBY

CAPTAIN, PITTSBURGH PENGUINS
BELFRY STUDENT: 2012–2015

DYNAMIC TRANSFORMATION

“I think he transformed my shot. I had a decent shot, but I never had the kind of dynamic skill set as far as that goes as I do now. I think a lot of credit goes to him with his work with me and that summer to get it ready to get it to the NHL level and be able to score in multiple different ways.”

AUSTON MATTHEWS

FORWARD, TORONTO MAPLE LEAFS
BELFRY STUDENT: SINCE 2016

SMOOTH TRANSITION

“Darryl really helped me transition from team to team. I’m really thankful I got in touch with him at that point because he did a great job of helping me acclimate to my new surroundings. I don’t think I would have been as successful in my ﬁrst few years in Dallas without having his input.”

JASON SPEZZA

FORWARD, TORONTO MAPLE LEAFS
BELFRY STUDENT: SINCE 2015

FROM BULL TO SPIDER

“Often he'll make a comment or I'll make a comment, and we'll kind of nod. Sometimes we don't even have to speak. We just look at each other and we know what we're trying to do. That's over 10 years working together and getting to know someone really well. I really respect him.”

HAYLEY WICKENHEISER

CAPTAIN, TEAM CANADA
BELFRY STUDENT: 2012–2015

EVERYTHING IS INTERCONNECTED
